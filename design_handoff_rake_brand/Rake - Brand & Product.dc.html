The idea

Rake is premium, trustworthy, and data-first — a scoreboard you can bet on. The identity earns that with restraint: warm paper, deep ink, one confident blue, and figures that always line up.

The mark

A dot-matrix R — the scoreboard, distilled

The R is drawn on a 5×7 dot grid — a nod to stadium boards and box scores, kept flat and calm (no glow, no neon). It carries the capital so the wordmark can stay lowercase.

{{ gridBlue }}
Construction. Five columns, seven rows, round dots on a fixed grid. Lit dots form the letter; the grid never changes — only the color and the dot size do.
